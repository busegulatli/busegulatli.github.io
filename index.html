Hi! I am Buse Gul Atli
I am a security researcher at Nokia Bell Labs in Finland.
My research is focused on trustworthy machine learning (ML). Trustworthy ML aims to ensure that ML-based services are fair, transparent in their decisions, and accountable while ensuring the security of its building blocks and the privacy of the ML model, data, and customers. I work on security, privacy, and governance aspects of trustworthy machine learning.
